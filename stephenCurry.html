Stephen Curry

Born March 14, 1988, is an American professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association (NBA). Widely regarded as the greatest shooter and one of the greatest players of all time.

Curry is the holder of numerous other records, notably for his efficiency and three-point shooting

  • ESPY Award for Best NBA Player 2016, 2021,2022
  • NBA Most Valuable Player 2021
  • Laureus World Sports Awards - Sportsman of the Year 2016,2017,2023
